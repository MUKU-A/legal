Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026  |  App: PicMap USA

This Privacy Policy explains how PicMap USA ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our app. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryDataSource
Account information Email address, display name (username), user ID Apple Sign In / Google Sign In
User content Photos you choose to upload Your device's photo library
Precise location GPS coordinates embedded in your photo's EXIF metadata Your uploaded photo
Device identifier IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), only if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission iOS (AdMob)

We do not collect real-time GPS location from your device. Location data is read only from EXIF metadata of photos you choose to upload, and is used solely to determine which U.S. state the photo was taken in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality – Authenticate you, display your posts on the map, enable voting, and manage your subscription.
  • Content display – Show your username alongside your photos to other users within the App. Top-ranked photos may be featured on a public gallery website; your username may appear with your photo.
  • Advertising – Serve interstitial ads via Google AdMob. If you grant ATT permission, the IDFA may be used for personalized advertising. If you deny ATT permission or subscribe to Pro, no personalized ads are shown.
  • Crash monitoring – Diagnose and fix technical issues using anonymized crash reports (Sentry).
  • Service improvement – Understand how users interact with the App to improve features and user experience.

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we ask for your permission before using the IDFA for advertising purposes (App Tracking Transparency prompt). If you deny permission, we will not use your IDFA, and you will see non-personalized ads (or no ads if you are a Pro subscriber). You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as follows:

  • With service providers listed in Section 3, solely to operate the App.
  • Publicly within the App – Your username and photos you post are visible to all App users. Top-ranked photos may be published on related external channels.
  • Legal requirements – If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account within the App. After deletion, your data is removed from our servers within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Photos you have posted will be removed from the App upon account deletion. If a photo was previously featured on an external channel, contact us to request removal.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase Row Level Security (RLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
