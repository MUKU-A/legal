Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

MyFish ("we", "us", "our") built the MyFish app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address, country, and date of birth — collected via Google / Apple sign-in
  • Fish photos you submit for 3D model generation
  • Support messages and file attachments you send us
  • Basic usage data (e.g., features used, crash logs)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the app
  • To generate 3D fish models from your photos
  • To respond to your support requests
  • For age verification
  • To serve advertisements (Free plan users)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — user authentication and data storage
  • Google / Apple — sign-in authentication
  • Third-party AI processing services — your fish photos are sent to these services solely to generate 3D models
  • Advertising networks — ads shown in the app may independently collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time via Settings → Delete Account, after which your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

5. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
