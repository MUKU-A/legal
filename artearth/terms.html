2. The Service

ARtEarth is an augmented reality social application that lets users place 3D works (GLB models, voxel models, 2D images, or AI‑generated models), photographs, paint strokes, and short messages at real‑world locations, and lets other users discover and view them in AR.

Key characteristics of the service include:

  • AR placements use Cloud Anchors that expire approximately 365 days after hosting unless re‑hosted by a contributor.
  • A separate "Supersize" placement program imposes a 60‑day approval window.
  • An AR paint feature stores ephemeral strokes locally and on our servers and automatically deletes them within a few hours.
  • A proximity chat feature is available within approximately 50 meters of an AR placement.
  • An optional AI generation feature converts a text prompt into a 3D model.

We may add, modify, or remove features at any time without notice.
